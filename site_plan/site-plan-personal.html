Fighter Duel

This is a application video game based on old fighter duels, but based in space. The basic premise is that an enemy fighter is heading straight for your station. You are sent out to stop them. You have three weapons choice, which each having pros and cons. You win if you defeat them, otherwise bad things happen. This is an based off of a game called Starship Commander.

Your Groceries, Your Budget

Summary: This is an app for those annoying situations where you have a $150 bucks to spend one groceries, and sales tax slips by you. The basic functionality will be to allow you to simply subtract prices from a determined budget with salex tax already applied. Bells and Whistles: Those imputs from the doing your shopping could be remembered. I plan on building a way to build lists based off of previous prices of products you have bought before. It could include which stores had what prices and where. I want to give more information for better choices to be made.
